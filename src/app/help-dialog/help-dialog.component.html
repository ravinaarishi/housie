How to play Bingo - Tambola - Housie

PREPARE TO PLAY

  • Distribute Tambola Tickets freely or at a fixed or mutually-agreed-upon price to everyone who wants to play.
  • Decide the prizes for the decided winning points. Winning points can be like:
    • Early 5: The ticket with first five struck numbers,
    • First Row/Top Row: The ticket with all numbers struck in a top row first,
    • Second Row/Middle Row: The ticket with all numbers struck in middle row first,
    • Third Row/Bottom Row: The ticket with all numbers struck in a bottom row first,
    • Corners: The ticket with all 4 corner numbers struck first (1st and last numbers of top and bottom rows),
    • Corner with Star: The ticket with all 4 corner & center-most numbers struck first (1st and last numbers of top and bottom rows along with a center-most number of the middle row),
    • Full House/First House: The ticket with all its numbers struck first,
    • Second House: The ticket with all its numbers struck second,
    • And So On…

PLAYING THE GAME

  • The CALLER should open the Housie Board by clicking on the button
  • The CALLER needs to click a the Next Number button to generate a random number to be called with a quirky message.
  • If a number called by CALLER exists on a player’s ticket, then the player has to strike/click it.
  • Once a particular Winning point is achieved by a PLAYER, he/she has to claim it immediately.
  • The CALLER has to verify the winning point by checking the struck numbers on the ticket with the called out numbers.
  • The CALLER then declares if the winning has been successfully claimed. If not, the winning point is still available to be claimed.
  • The game ends when all the FULL HOUSEs are successfully CLAIMED.
